Privacy Policy

Spirit Box: Ghost Detector EVP — Last Updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Spirit Box: Ghost Detector EVP mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

The App is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All sensor data (magnetometer readings) is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server.

Sensor Data

The App uses your device's magnetometer (compass sensor) to detect electromagnetic field fluctuations. This data is processed locally on your device in real time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted. No sensor data leaves your device.

Audio Playback

The App plays pre-recorded audio clips stored locally within the App. No audio is recorded from your environment. No microphone access is required or used.

Session Recording

If you choose to record a session, the recording is stored locally on your device. You control whether to save, share, or delete it. We never access your recordings.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).

Subscriptions

The App offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Subscription management and billing are handled by Apple. For details, see Apple's Terms and Conditions.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools beyond what is provided by AdMob and Apple's standard App Store analytics.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Entertainment Disclaimer

The App is designed for entertainment purposes only. It does not actually detect ghosts, spirits, or paranormal activity. Electromagnetic readings shown are real sensor data but should not be interpreted as evidence of supernatural phenomena.

Changes to This Policy
